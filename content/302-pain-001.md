> **The problem first.** Bob taps "send" in his banking app. That tap has to become something his bank can act on. What does the app actually transmit, and how is it different from the message his bank sends onward?

**This deep dive is being written.** It will break down **pain.001** (*Payments Initiation*) — the customer-to-bank credit transfer request — including its three levels (`GrpHdr`, `PmtInf`, `CdtTrfTxInf`) and exactly where Bob's bank takes over and emits the bank-to-bank [pacs.008](#).

`pain` = *Payments Initiation*. The mirror image, `pain.002`, carries the status back.
